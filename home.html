TO EDIT

 

Electronics' market has been flooded with many portable batteries because they are easy to design & cheap to manufacture.

 

If you choose to spend a little extra on a power bank from a recognizable brand then you can expect two things.

• Battery quality should be better. This means that over time, batery's capacity loss will be slower and battery will last longer. Sadly, all batteries lose capacity over time.

• Battery capacity should be equal to what is advertised. It happens that non-branded power banks advertise falsely, and after testings they prove to carry less battery capacity than they are supposed to.
